SprayScape is a spatial-computing platform designed to archive and authenticate urban graffiti using AR visualization and blockchain attribution. The system integrates geolocation anchors and digital certificates to ensure long-term stability and authorship verification, highlighting strategies for cultural computing and socio-technical systems that link digital heritage with sustainable design.
